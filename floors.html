About Me

First Floor

When you enter the house. On the left you will see a couple of trees and plants. After moving a bit forward you will see 2 doors. The left door will be the drawing Room and the other is the restroom. You will see a big lawn, after moving forward. And then two big doors. When you will enter the door you will see four doors. On the right side will be the drawing room and on the left will be the living room. And adjecent will be my bedroom beside my bedroom will be my uncle's bedroom. besides the living room will be the kitchen and beside the kitchen will be the store room.

Ground Floor

In the lawn you will see stairs, which will be leading to the ground floor. On the Ground Floor you will see a gate and when you enter it, you will see a kitchen on the right and besides those you'll see 4 rooms. 1rst room will be a room with two beds nobody lives there, the next my grandparent's room, the next the drawing room and my aunt's room and adjecent my uncle's room.

The Green Grass

This is an area of grass and flowers at the front of the house. It consists flowers like rose, lily, sunflower etc, and many types of bugs live in here e.g grasshopper, spider, scorpions(in a little quantity), beetle and so much more.
